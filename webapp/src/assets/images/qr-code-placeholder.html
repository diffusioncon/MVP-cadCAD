QR Code Generator

QR codes are a quick way to interact with customers and have many creative marketing uses. Create your own QR codes in minutes with our generator.

Improve Marketing and Guide Customers with QR Codes

QR codes are a convenient way to link customers to online resources using a printed image on a product, business card, or other physical item. The customer simply scans the QR code with their smartphone and the relevant link is opened in their mobile browser. QR codes are a great form of interactive marketing as they can send customers directly to a promotion, social media profile, or anything else within seconds — and with no typing on the customer's part. 3dcart has created a powerful QR code generator that can create QR codes with a variety of different functions.
